---
title: Japanese Concepts
---

1. Ikigai- discover purpose/reason to wake-up
2. Kaisen- small improvements each day
3. Pomodoro technique- 25 min. without distraction
4. Hara Hachi Bu- not too much food
5. Shoshin- beginner approach
6. Wabi Sabi- embrace imperfection
7. Shinrin-Yoku- bathe in nature
8. Ganbaru- max effort and perseverence; do better than one's best
9. Gaman- endure the seemingly unbearable with patience and dignity
10. Oubaitori- don't compare to others; own path
11. Yugen- profound, mysterious sense of the beauty of the universe; feeling of beauty
12. Kanso- simplicity
13. Shizen- aligning with nature or it's rhythms
14. Seijaku- active calm
15. Datsuzoku- breaking routine; intentional deviation
